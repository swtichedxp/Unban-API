Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the common reasons for a WhatsApp ban?
  • Using unauthorized apps (WhatsApp Plus, GB WhatsApp, etc.).
  • Sending bulk or automated messages (spamming).
  • Creating fake accounts or impersonating others.
  • Being blocked or reported by many users.
  • Violating terms of service, including sending illegal or harmful content.
  • What is the difference between a temporary and permanent ban?
  • A **temporary ban** is a short-term suspension that usually lifts after a set period. It's often a warning for a minor violation.
  • A **permanent ban** means your account is blocked indefinitely and cannot be recovered unless you win a formal appeal.
  • What should I do after my appeal is sent?
  • Wait patiently for a response. Do not send multiple appeals in a short period, as this may be seen as spam and harm your case.
  • Can I get unbanned even with a permanent ban?
  • Yes, but it is challenging. The goal of this tool is to help you craft a professional appeal that a human reviewer will see and consider.
