Frequently Asked Questions

What are the common reasons for a WhatsApp ban?

The most frequent reasons include:
• Using unauthorized apps like WhatsApp Plus or GB WhatsApp.
• Sending bulk messages or spam.
• Creating fake accounts or impersonating others.
• Being reported or blocked by a large number of users.
• Violating the terms of service, such as sharing illegal or harmful content.

What's the difference between a temporary and permanent ban?

A **temporary ban** is a short-term suspension that usually lifts on its own after a set period. It's often a warning for a minor violation. A **permanent ban** means your account is blocked indefinitely and requires a successful formal appeal to be recovered.

What should I do after sending my appeal?

Wait patiently for a response from WhatsApp Support. Do not send multiple appeals in a short period, as this can be seen as spam and may hurt your case.

Can I get unbanned from a permanent ban?

Yes, but it is challenging. This tool is designed to help you craft a professional and effective appeal that increases your chances of a human reviewer seeing and considering your request.
